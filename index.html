What We Do

Several years ago, we had a dream and decided to venture out, take risks, and invest in this beautiful business. We loved it from the very first moment; we enjoyed working with the people around us, creating spaces, combining colors and textures, falling in love with serving our customers, and above all, we loved having dreams.

Design and Innovation

We travel the world to bring the latest designs, trends, and novelties to the country. This way, we constantly renew our product offerings. Innovating means that we always offer you the latest designs at affordable prices.

Immediate Availability

The furniture and decorative elements you want, right when you want them. We know you like to renovate without wasting time, so at HomeDecor, we don't make you wait weeks or months. If the product is available, you take it immediately, with the exception of some furniture for which delivery coordination is needed.

Service and Installation

Leave the assembly of the furniture in the hands of our experts, ensuring that the product is perfectly installed for your enjoyment. Wherever you are, our assemblers will visit you.
